Chocolate Cake

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classsic Chocolate Cake Recipe. Enjoy!"


Difficulty Prep Time Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

grab your list of ingredients

2

Mix everything together in a bowl

3

place in baking pan

4

bake at 400 for 25 min
